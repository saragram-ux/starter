# Starter

A simple starting point for building websites.

Clean defaults.
Simple structure.
Nothing extra.

---

## Why Starter?

Most setups are either too empty or too complex.

Starter sits in between.

It gives you just enough structure to begin without getting in your way.

---

## What’s included

- Layout (containers, sections, spacing)
- Typography (headings, text styles)
- Components (buttons, cards, forms)
- Utilities (basic helpers)

---

## Example

```html
<section class="page-padding section-large">
  <div class="container-medium">
    <h1 class="heading-h1">Hello</h1>
    <p class="text-medium text-muted">A clean starting point.</p>
  </div>
</section>
```

---

## Philosophy

Keep it simple.

If something feels complicated, it probably is.

---

## Use it your way

Starter is not a framework.

Take what you need.
Change what you don’t.
